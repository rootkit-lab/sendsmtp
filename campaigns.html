Campaign examples

Subjects and HTML snippets for payment confirmations and invoices / notas fiscais. Placeholders: {{email}}, {{link}}, {{assunto}}, {{from}}, {{uniq}}. Spintax uses {a|b|c}.

pt-BR pt-PT es-AR es-ES es-MX en-US it-IT fr-FR

How to use

  1. Copy subject lines into data/assuntos.txt (one per line).
  2. Copy an HTML body into data/msg.html.
  3. Put base URLs in data/links.txt — do not add ?p=; SendSMTP appends ?p=<email> to every {{link}}.
Ready-to-paste files also live under website/examples/ in the repo.
