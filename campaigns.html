Campaign examples

Full HTML templates for payment confirmations and invoices / notas fiscais, with live preview. Placeholders: {{email}} {{link}} {{assunto}} {{from}} {{uniq}}. Spintax: {a|b|c}.

Preview resolves spintax to the first option and fills sample placeholders. Copied HTML keeps {{…}} and {a|b} intact for SendSMTP.
